A Few Movie Recomendations

Three of my Favorite Movies

How to Lose a Guy in 10 Days

A classic 90's rom-com that follows Andie as she tried to write an article on the typical actions that cause a guy to end the relationship.

Castle in the Sky

A Studio Ghibli movie that follows Sheeta as she tries to keep a powerful crystal from her kidnapper.

Moulin Rouge!

A poet falls in love with an aspiring actress from the Moulin Rouge. They try to keep their relationship a secret as she is promised to a wealthy Duke.
